Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), Amritsar: This iconic Sikh gurdwara is known for its stunning architecture, gilded exterior, and spiritual atmosphere. It's a place of pilgrimage and equality for all, regardless of background.
Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, Tirupati: Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (Vishnu), this temple is one of the richest and most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.
Meenakshi Temple, Madurai: A historic temple complex dedicated to Goddess Parvati (Meenakshi) and her consort, Lord Shiva. It's famous for its towering gopurams (gateway towers) adorned with intricate sculptures.
Shree Jagannath Temple, Puri: Dedicated to Lord Jagannath (Vishnu), this temple is a major pilgrimage site in eastern India and is known for its annual Rath Yatra (chariot festival).
